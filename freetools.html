Free 3D Modeling Tools

Create your own 3D models with these free tools before sending them to PrintHub.cc for printing.

MakerWorld Tools

Ready-to-print customizable tools that let you create personalized models in minutes without any 3D modeling experience.

MakeMySign

Create custom signs and text-based 3D models in seconds. Perfect for name plates, door signs, labels, and decorative text.

  • No design skills needed
  • Multiple fonts and styles
  • Instant 3D model generation
Use MakeMySign
MakerBox

Design custom boxes with lids, compartments, and dimensions tailored to your needs.

  • Adjustable sizing
  • Various lid types
  • STL ready to download
Use MakerBox
MakerLithophane

Turn any photo into a stunning 3D lithophane. Ideal for night lights and custom gifts.

  • Upload photo, auto-generate STL
  • Circular, flat, and curved options
  • Light-reactive effects
Use MakerLithophane
MakerKeychain - Image to keychain

Design personalized keychains with custom text, shapes, and styles.

  • Customizable fonts & colors
  • Multiple keyring options
  • STL export in one click
Use MakerKeychain
Make My Desk Organizer

Design your own custom desk organizer with compartments tailored to your workspace. Keep pens, tools, or gadgets neatly arranged.

  • Modular compartments
  • Custom sizing and layout
  • Printable in one click
Use Desk Organizer Tool

3D Modeling Software

Full-featured 3D modeling applications for creating custom designs from scratch.

Tinkercad

A free, easy-to-use web app for 3D design, ideal for beginners. Perfect for creating simple models without any prior 3D modeling experience.

  • Browser-based (no installation)
  • Perfect for beginners
  • Simple shapes & combining tools
Visit Tinkercad
Blender

A powerful, professional-grade 3D creation suite supporting the entirety of the 3D pipeline. Completely free and open source.

  • Professional-grade features
  • Extensive modeling capabilities
  • Active community with tutorials
Visit Blender
FreeCAD

A parametric 3D modeler made primarily for designing real-life objects of any size. Especially suited for engineering and mechanical design.

  • Parametric modeling approach
  • Great for technical designs
  • Precise measurements & dimensions
Visit FreeCAD
SketchUp Free

A web-based 3D modeling software with an intuitive interface, making it easy to create architectural models, interior designs, and more.

  • Intuitive interface
  • Great for architectural models
  • Web-based, no download needed
Visit SketchUp Free
OpenSCAD

A script-only based modeler that uses its own description language to create 3D models. Perfect for those who prefer to code their designs.

  • Code-based 3D modeling
  • Parametric design approach
  • Perfect for technical parts
Visit OpenSCAD
Fusion 360 Personal

Professional 3D CAD, CAM, and CAE tools for product development, available free for personal use with some limitations.

  • Professional-grade CAD
  • Free for personal use
  • Cloud-based collaboration
Get Fusion 360

Getting Started Tips

New to 3D modeling? Here are some recommendations:

  • Complete Beginners: Start with Tinkercad or MakerWorld tools for simple projects
  • Learning CAD: Try FreeCAD or Fusion 360 Personal for technical designs
  • Artistic Projects: Blender is excellent for creative and artistic 3D models
  • Programmers: OpenSCAD lets you code your 3D designs
